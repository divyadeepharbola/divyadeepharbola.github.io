My PhD

The PhD project focuses on investigating chemical weathering processes through a weathering experiment, followed by the dating of weathered products using different analytical techniques. The research will involve advanced analytical techniques, such as X-ray diffraction, Scanning Electron Microscopy, and Radiometric dating, to provide comprehensive insights into the geological and environmental processes shaping the Western Ghats.
